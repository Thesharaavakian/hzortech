When reaching out

Helpful things to include

We can give you a more useful first response if your message covers:

  • A brief summary of the problem or goal
  • Key systems or platforms involved (e.g. AWS, on-prem Windows, Kubernetes)
  • Rough timeline or any hard deadlines
  • Whether this is a one-off project or ongoing support

No formal brief needed — a few lines is fine.

How we evaluate work

Our evaluation process

When a new project comes in, we look at:

  1. Scope and what the actual goal is (not just the stated deliverable)
  2. Current infrastructure and security posture
  3. Integration requirements — APIs, payments, enterprise systems
  4. Any compliance or regulatory constraints

We'll be straightforward if we're not the right fit for something.
