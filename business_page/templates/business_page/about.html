About

Built on real engineering experience

HzorTech was founded by Shara Avakian — a cloud and cybersecurity engineer who spent years working on live banking systems, high-performance compute infrastructure, and large-scale endpoint security before starting this.

Mission

Deliver practical, secure technology services to small and medium businesses that need cloud and security expertise but don't have a dedicated team for it.

Vision

Become a trusted long-term technical partner for clients in finance, research, and enterprise IT — sectors where reliability and security aren't optional.
