About

Built on real engineering experience

HzorTech was founded by Shara Avakian — an engineer who spent years working on live banking systems, high-performance compute infrastructure, and enterprise security before starting this. The services we offer are the ones we've delivered under pressure in production environments.

Mission

To help businesses digitize, automate, and secure their operations by building reliable software systems, connecting their tools, and ensuring their infrastructure runs efficiently with minimal manual work.

Vision

Become the go-to technical partner for growing businesses that need software built, workflows automated, and infrastructure secured — without the cost of a full internal team. We want clients to build on what we deliver, long after the engagement ends.

How we work

Operating principles

Automation first

Every engagement gets reviewed for manual processes that can be eliminated. We look for the toil before adding headcount.

Security by default

Security controls are built in from the start — access control, logging, and hardening are not optional extras.

Direct communication

You talk to the engineers working on your project. No account managers in the middle, no filtered status updates.

Documentation and handover

Every engagement includes runbooks and knowledge transfer. Your team should be able to maintain what we build without calling us.

Measured outcomes

We agree on success metrics before starting and report against them. No moving goalposts, no vague deliverables.

Continuous improvement

Delivery doesn't stop at launch. Retainer clients get regular reviews and a steady cadence of improvements — not just reactive support.
